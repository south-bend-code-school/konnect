About

Konnect was created to give the homeless a better ability to attend the community and to give the community better ways to inform the homeless about the different organizations who will advocate their needs and task for certain things. With the konnect app you will instantly know about the community and what going on. Subscribe to the community and expand your knowledge of those in need.
